### whoami

ibot. polymath. broke. building anyway.

i don't do prototypes. i don't do proof of concepts. i do the real thing or i don't do it at all. if something's worth building it's worth building properly with documentation and everything and all the details that make it actually good instead of just functional.

i make therapeutic games for kids navigating trauma (ACT-based, trauma-informed, no wrong answers). i build IRC infrastructure because draconic is awesome and the old internet was rad actually. i make music that sounds like grief processed through a terminal window. i do all of it with the same energy because they're not different things, they're the same thing expressed differently.

a lot of this exists because of josh. he was the love of my life. his signal is in everything i finish.
